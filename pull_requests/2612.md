## Summary

Add [Monero One](https://monero.one) to the Mobile & Light Wallets section under Local Sync.

Monero One is a free, open-source, non-custodial Monero wallet for iOS and Android that connects directly to Monero nodes via RPC.

- **iOS:** [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/monero-one/id6757898541)
- **Android:** Beta available at [monero.one](https://monero.one)
- **Source code:** [github.com/bwgh0/MoneroOne](https://github.com/bwgh0/MoneroOne)
