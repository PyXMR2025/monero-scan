# About Artificial Intelligence and digital currencies
author: tinysouth
created_at: 2024-05-11T13:12:02Z
status: closed

It is believed that digital currencies such as Bitcoin and Litecoin are unnecessary, and the mining and earning of these currencies consume a large amount of human actual energy, resources, and computing power, resulting in only strings with no actual value or meaning. Using such strings as currency to measure value is not as good as using a certain indicator in the current development of artificial intelligence to act as a digital currency, measure value, and empower the development of artificial intelligence. It is recommended to replace traditional meaningless digital currencies and redefine and develop digital currencies using a certain indicator in the training of artificial intelligence generation models or a certain indicator during the operation of artificial intelligence.
