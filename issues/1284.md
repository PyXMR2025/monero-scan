# MoneroKon 2026 in Serbia
author: recanman
created_at: 2025-10-24T08:16:09Z
status: closed

The current organizing team voted to cancel MoneroKon 2026, and [subsequently announced the cancellation here](https://matrix.to/#/!QTthKnkZZSKAUufchb:matrix.org/$oF71cOhCsrRnTL1pAcBU9gyqymhMkQTkD-OmisIdkv4?via=matrix.org&via=monero.social&via=unredacted.org) on September 20, 2025. Note: I am a staff member of this organizing team.

This was due to various reasons: legal, current organizing team being tired, etc. Additionally, the release of the [new EU digital border system that mandates collection of biometrics for non-EU citizens](https://commission.europa.eu/news-and-media/news/how-new-digital-borders-system-works-2025-10-13_en) excludes individuals that do not want to give up their biometric information. We were previously organizing in Prague, Czech Republic; my effort is organizing in Serbia, [which is not part of the Schengen area](https://visaguide.world/schengen-visa/schengen-area-countries-list/), thus exempt from the EU digital border system.

MoneroKon is a community effort, with no "owner"; as such, any individual can attempt to organize MoneroKon. This is what I am attempting to do. I have been in contact with a few individuals involved in Liberland; their [ARK Village in Serbia](https://anniversary.ll.land/travel-guide/) appears to be an appropriate venue, and they are happy to host MoneroKon there. We have tentative dates for June 19-21, 2026.

Before moving forward with organization, I am calling for a meeting on October 25, at 16:00 UTC, for any discussion regarding this matter, to take place in the [MoneroKon Matrix channel](https://matrix.to/#/#monerokon:matrix.org).
