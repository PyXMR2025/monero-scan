author: Kanopola
created_at: 2024-04-10T03:53:36Z
status: open

¡Hola!

Por favor, sugiero que sea colocado el "Minado Automático" en la App Monero Gui Wallet para Windows, ya que, sucede que a veces cuando uno reinicia o apaga la computadora, aunque tenga el minero que inicie de forma automática, este no empieza a minar porque después que sincroniza con el Nodo y la Billetera sincroniza los bloques, hay que darle clic donde dice "empezar a minar" para que empiece.

![Screenshot_13](https://github.com/monero-project/monero-gui/assets/56226178/0ebc9548-7a79-48cf-9455-3b39ac89389b)

Espero que lo implementen para la próxima actualización, ya que, estoy seguro que la comunidad lo va a recibir con gran emoción.

Hello!

Please, I suggest that "Automatic Mining" be placed in the Monero Gui Wallet App for Windows, since it happens that sometimes when you restart or turn off the computer, even if you have the miner that starts automatically, it does not start mining because after it synchronizes with the Node and the Wallet synchronizes the blocks, you have to click where it says "start mining" for it to start.

The essential problem is that, since sometimes you have to wait a few minutes for everything to synchronize, you entertain yourself looking at other things and forget; and in most cases, when one comes to remember, several hours have passed, and that is a loss of power for the network and benefits for the User.

I hope they implement it for the next update, since I am sure the community will receive it with great excitement.
