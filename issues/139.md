# Add New Option: Entrepreneurship
author: RunnyBabbit
created_at: 2025-08-15T06:09:53Z
status: closed

The solution to a 51% attack by a malicious state or finance actor, or a consolidated pool, is grow the network, and also grow p2pool — with entrepreneurialism

RandomX CPU mining is an untapped resource, and we should act like it.

Here are viable business models for opt-in proxy-mining:

**Business Idea #1** — Create an on-demand media-streaming platform that requires a minimum amount of p2pool hash to stream any pirated content you want, on demand, for ½ the price of just one streaming service

First, bootstrap the laptop market as a Monero-mining desktop app to enable streaming content from virtual servers; you wouldn't even need to set up servers in your home, as you could scale the virtual servers up & down based on demand

Step 2, with some of the profits, design an open-source arduino television box, to break into the laptop-less demographic/market; make the design simple enough that any hobbyist can build cheaply, or assemble and start a business selling on xmrbazaar

The laprop app & television boxes pays you to host on-demand streaming of pirated content from HBO, Netflix, Spotify, etc from your media server over Tor or i2p
