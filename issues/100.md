# Correct coinbase-emission confusion in get_coinbase_tx_sum
author: Rucknium
created_at: 2024-11-26T15:13:59Z
status: closed

"Coinbase" is the sum of new coins emitted and all transaction fees: https://www.getmonero.org/resources/moneropedia/coinbase.html
Emission is just the new coins: https://www.getmonero.org/resources/moneropedia/tail-emission.html
